Why Most Coaching Books Fail

The book doesn't lead anywhere

Most coaches dump everything they know onto pages. No structure. No path. The reader finishes and thinks "that was nice" — and moves on. No call. No client. No money.

Your book needs to be built so the reader's last thought is "I need to work with this person."

Nobody ever sees it

Malcolm Gladwell never ran a single ad for his books. Tony Robbins doesn't run ads. They get people with big audiences to promote them. Most coaches skip this entirely. They put the book on Amazon and wait.

Author vs BESTSELLING Author

You can get a book on Amazon. But "an author" means nothing — there are over a million of them. A BESTSELLING author? That changes everything. That's what got Mynoo to the United Nations. That's what turned Aila from unknown to $200K in contracts.
